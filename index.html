Classic Arepas
PAN White Corn Flour

Soft, round corn cakes stuffed with melted cheese, chicken avocado, black beans or butter. A daily staple in Venezuelan and Colombian homes.

You need: 2 cups PAN White Flour · 2.5 cups warm water · 1 tsp salt · 1 tbsp oil
  1. Mix flour, salt and water until smooth. Rest 5 min.
  2. Shape into palm-sized balls, flatten to ~1cm thick rounds.
  3. Cook on medium pan 5–7 min each side until golden.
  4. Finish in oven 180°C for 10 min for fluffy inside.
  5. Cut open and fill with cheese, avocado, chicken or beans.

Hallacas
PAN Yellow Corn Flour

Traditional Venezuelan Christmas dish — spiced meat wrapped in corn dough and banana leaves, then boiled. Rich, festive and full of flavour.

You need: 2 cups PAN Yellow Flour · warm water · chicken/pork filling · banana leaves · salt · annatto oil
  1. Cook chicken or pork with onion, garlic and spices. Let cool.
  2. Mix yellow flour with warm water, salt and annatto oil until smooth.
  3. Soften banana leaves over flame. Cut into rectangles.
  4. Spread thin dough on leaf. Add filling in centre.
  5. Fold banana leaf tightly and tie with string.
  6. Boil in salted water 45–60 min. Serve hot.

Corn Empanadas
PAN White or Yellow Flour

Crispy golden fried pockets filled with seasoned meat and melted cheese. A favourite snack in Venezuela and Colombia.

You need: 2 cups PAN Flour · 2 cups warm water · 1 tsp salt · meat & cheese filling · oil for frying
  1. Mix flour, water and salt into smooth dough. Rest 5 min.
  2. Flatten small dough ball into thin circle on a plastic bag.
  3. Place meat and cheese on one half.
  4. Fold into half-moon, press edges firmly to seal.
  5. Deep fry at 180°C for 3–4 min until golden crispy.
  6. Serve with hot sauce, avocado or lemon.

Colombian Tamales
PAN Yellow Corn Flour

Hearty corn parcels filled with chicken, pork and vegetables, wrapped in banana leaves. A beloved Sunday morning classic in Colombia.

You need: 2 cups PAN Yellow Flour · chicken · potato · carrot · peas · banana leaves · salt · cumin
  1. Season chicken with salt, cumin and garlic. Cook until half-done. Cube vegetables.
  2. Mix flour with warm water, salt and oil into soft dough.
  3. Spread dough 1cm thick on softened banana leaf.
  4. Add chicken, potato, carrot and peas on top.
  5. Fold banana leaf tightly and tie with string.
  6. Steam or boil 60–90 min. Serve hot.

Mazamorra (Corn Porridge)
PAN Sweet Corn Mix Flour

A warm, comforting sweet porridge — a beloved Colombian dessert with cinnamon. Great for breakfast or after dinner. Kids love it!

You need: 4 tbsp PAN Sweet Flour · 2 cups milk · 2 tbsp sugar · cinnamon to taste
  1. Dissolve sweet corn flour in a little cold milk to avoid lumps.
  2. Heat remaining milk in saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Add flour mixture, stirring constantly.
  4. Add sugar and cinnamon. Stir until thick (5–8 min).
  5. Pour into bowls, sprinkle extra cinnamon on top. Serve warm.

Sweet Corn Cake (Torta de Maíz)
PAN Sweet Corn Mix Flour

A moist and naturally sweet South American corn cake — simple to bake and perfect for afternoon tea or dessert.

You need: 1.5 cups PAN Sweet Flour · 2 eggs · 1 cup milk · ½ cup sugar · ½ cup butter · 1 tsp baking powder
  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease baking tin.
  2. Beat eggs, sugar and melted butter together.
  3. Add milk and mix well.
  4. Stir in flour and baking powder until smooth batter.
  5. Bake 30–35 min until golden. Cool 10 min before slicing.

Cachapas con Queso
PAN Sweet Corn Mix Flour 500g

Venezuelan corn pancakes folded with melted queso de mano (mozzarella) and butter. Sweet, soft and absolutely delicious!

You need: 1 cup PAN Sweet Corn Mix · ½ cup milk · 1 egg · 1 tbsp butter · pinch of salt · mozzarella cheese
  1. Mix sweet corn flour, milk, egg, butter and salt into smooth batter.
  2. Heat non-stick pan on medium with a little butter.
  3. Pour a ladleful of batter, spread into round pancake shape.
  4. Cook 3–4 min until bubbles appear, flip and cook 2–3 more min.
  5. Place mozzarella on one half and fold over.
  6. Serve immediately while cheese is warm and melted!

Gluten Free Corn Churros
PAN White Corn Flour

Crispy golden churros made with PAN pre-cooked corn flour — naturally gluten free and dusted with cinnamon sugar. Easy to make at home.

You need: 2 cups PAN White Corn Flour · 2.5 cups warm water · 3 tbsp sugar · 1 tsp salt · 2 tbsp butter · oil for frying · cinnamon sugar to coat
  1. Heat the water with sugar, salt and butter until warm and the butter melts.
  2. Gradually stir in the PAN corn flour until a soft, smooth dough forms. Rest 5 min.
  3. Knead lightly until pliable — add a splash of water if dry, a little flour if sticky.
  4. Spoon into a piping bag with a star tip and pipe 10–12 cm strips, cutting with scissors.
  5. Fry in oil at 180°C for 3–4 min, turning, until golden and crisp. Drain on paper towels.
  6. Roll in cinnamon sugar while warm. Serve with chocolate or dulce de leche. Enjoy!

We are a small South American business LA ESQUINA LATINA LTD based in Auckland, New Zealand. Our mission is to bring the authentic taste of home to the Latin community and to anyone in New Zealand who wishes to explore the wonderful world of P.A.N. Flour cooking.
